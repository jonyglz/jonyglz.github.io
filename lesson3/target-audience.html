Audience

[The website is directed to everyone in the Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. It would also be useful for tourists planning to visit.]

Personas

[The first personas that I could identify for this site after studying the main occupations in Preston, ID were the sales workers. They are the biggest sector in Preston. They being people that are usually working on a 9-5 shift. It is important for them to see the weather mainly if they work on door sales.]

[The second type of personas based on the datausa.io website for Soda Springs are the manufacturing workers. However, there is a portion of the population that does mining, quarrying and oil and gas extraction. So it would be importat for them to know if the weather is favourable for their extraction duties.]

Scenarios

  • I am visiting Preston for the weekend. What is the weather like throughout my hike?
  • I do door sales. What is the best time to go so that there is not a lot of sun or raining?
  • I work on the mining industry and a storm could potentially create floods in the mine. Can the site tell me the probability of a storm on a daily baisis?
  • I work on agriculture and I am trying to place the seeds this weekend. Can I get an accurate prediction of the weather?
  • During witner there is a lot of snow. Can I get to know how the roads are?
